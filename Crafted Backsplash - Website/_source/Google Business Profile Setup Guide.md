# Google Business Profile — Setup Guide for Crafted Backsplash

Your Google Business Profile (GBP) is the single highest-impact SEO action you can take. It's what puts you in the **map pack** (the 3-pack of local businesses that appear above organic results when someone searches "backsplash installer Omaha"). Without it, you don't exist in local search.

---

## Step 1 — Claim or Create Your Profile

Go to **business.google.com** and sign in with a Google account you own.

- Search "Crafted Backsplash" to see if a profile already exists. If it does, click **Claim this business**.
- If nothing exists, click **Add your business** and work through the setup wizard.

---

## Step 2 — Fill Out Every Field

Incomplete profiles rank lower. Fill in all of the following:

**Business name:** `Crafted Backsplash`
*(Do NOT stuff keywords into the name — "Crafted Backsplash — Tile Installer Omaha" violates Google's guidelines and can get you suspended.)*

**Category:** Primary → `Tile Contractor`
Add secondary categories: `Kitchen Remodeler`, `Home Improvement Contractor`

**Address:** Use your business address. If you operate without a storefront, select **Service area business** and hide your address — then list your service areas instead.

**Service areas:** Add all of these:
- Omaha, NE
- Lincoln, NE
- Bellevue, NE
- Papillion, NE
- La Vista, NE
- Gretna, NE
- Elkhorn, NE
- Fremont, NE

**Phone number:** Add your primary business number.

**Website:** `https://craftedbacksplash.com`

**Hours:** Set your actual working hours. Leaving this blank hurts ranking.

---

## Step 3 — Write the Business Description

Google gives you 750 characters. Use all of them. Here's a ready-to-paste version:

> Crafted Backsplash specializes in professional kitchen tile backsplash installation across Omaha, Lincoln, and the surrounding Nebraska metro. We install subway tile, natural stone, herringbone, chevron, and custom patterns — with the precision and accountability that has earned our parent company Crafted Performance Homes a 5-star rating. Licensed, insured Nebraska LLC. Get a free rough estimate online in 2 minutes at craftedbacksplash.com — no contractor visit or email required to start.

---

## Step 4 — Add Your Services

In the **Products/Services** section, add:

- **Kitchen Tile Backsplash Installation** — Full-service install: demo, prep, tile, grout, caulk, and cleanup.
- **Free Online Backsplash Estimate** — Rough price range in 2 minutes at craftedbacksplash.com.
- **In-Home Measure & Quote** — Firm itemized quote after a quick in-home measure. Price locked before we start.

---

## Step 5 — Upload Photos

Photos are heavily weighted in local ranking. Upload at least 10 project photos from your existing folder.

**Photo categories to cover:**
- At least 3 completed kitchen backsplash photos (wide shots showing the full kitchen)
- At least 2 detail/close-up shots (grout lines, corner work, edge treatments)
- 1 logo photo (use your Crafted Backsplash logo PNG)
- 1 cover photo (your best wide-angle project shot — something bright and impressive)

**Naming tip:** Before uploading, rename files to descriptive names like `white-subway-tile-backsplash-omaha-ne.jpg`. Google reads file names.

---

## Step 6 — Get Reviews (Most Important Ongoing Task)

Reviews are the #1 local ranking factor. The best time to ask is immediately after the job is done while the homeowner is happy.

**How to ask:**
1. In GBP, go to **Home → Get more reviews** and copy your review link.
2. Save it as a short link (use bit.ly or just bookmark it).
3. Text it to the homeowner the same day as installation: *"Really appreciate the opportunity — if you have 2 minutes, a Google review goes a long way for a small business: [link]"*

**Target:** Get to 10+ reviews as fast as possible. The map pack almost always shows businesses with reviews.

---

## Step 7 — Submit Your Sitemap to Google Search Console

This gets your pages indexed faster.

1. Go to **search.google.com/search-console** and add your property (`craftedbacksplash.com`).
2. Verify ownership (Google will walk you through this — easiest method is HTML tag or DNS record).
3. Once verified, go to **Sitemaps** and submit: `https://craftedbacksplash.com/sitemap.xml`

---

## Step 8 — Keep It Active (Ongoing)

Google rewards active profiles:

- **Post updates monthly.** GBP has a "Posts" feature — treat it like a minimal social feed. Post a before/after photo, announce availability, share a tip. Even one post per month helps.
- **Respond to every review** — especially negative ones. Professional responses signal to Google (and potential customers) that the business is engaged.
- **Add Q&As.** You can pre-populate the Questions & Answers section yourself. Add the same questions from your site FAQ (cost range, service area, timeline, tile supply).

---

## Priority Order

If you're short on time, do these first:

1. Claim/create the profile and fill out every field *(30 min)*
2. Upload 10 photos *(15 min)*
3. Get your review link and start asking for reviews *(5 min)*
4. Set up Google Search Console and submit sitemap *(20 min)*
5. Posts and Q&As can come later

The map pack is where local service businesses get most of their clicks. This is worth doing before anything else in the SEO roadmap.
